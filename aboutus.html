Our Mission

At LeLink, we design vehicle diagnostic solutions that are simple, fast, and reliable. Our flagship product, the LeLink OBD-II Adapter, brings professional-grade engine monitoring and diagnostics directly to your smartphone—without complicated setup or confusing tools.

What is the LeLink OBD Adapter?

The LeLink adapter is a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) OBD-II scanner built specifically for iPhone and iPad users. When paired with apps like Engine Link, it gives real-time access to engine data, error codes, sensor readings, fuel efficiency details, and more. It is widely recognized as one of the most stable and power-efficient OBD-II adapters for iOS.

Why Drivers Choose LeLink

  • Zero standby power – The adapter automatically turns off to protect your car battery.
  • Fast, stable BLE connection – No pairing required; simply plug in and open the app.
  • Advanced customization – Supports custom PIDs, formulas, Mode 6 data, batch mode, and more.
  • Secure operation – Optional password protection via the free LeLink Security app.
  • Developer friendly – Sample iOS and Android code available for integrating OBD functions.

How It Works

Getting started is simple:

  1. Turn on Bluetooth on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Plug the LeLink adapter into your car’s OBD-II port.
  3. Open the Engine Link app—your adapter connects automatically.

Once connected, you can monitor live sensors, read/clear trouble codes, create custom dashboards, and even add user-defined PIDs for vehicle-specific data.

For Power Users

LeLink supports advanced features including:

  • Custom PID files (.csv)
  • ELM327 command testing and configuration
  • Batch mode for rapid data requests
  • Editable startup sequences (obdcmd.ini)
  • Raw data logging for diagnostics

Whether you're a car enthusiast, developer, or mechanic, LeLink provides the flexibility you need to work with OBD-II data on a deeper level.
