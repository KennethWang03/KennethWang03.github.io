What is LELink?

LELink is a professional-grade OBD-II adapter designed for vehicle diagnostics, performance tracking, and real-time engine monitoring. Whether you're an automotive enthusiast or a professional mechanic, LELink makes it easy to scan and clear engine codes, monitor live data, and customize your vehicle insights.

Key Features

  • User-configurable auto on/off with ZERO standby power.
  • Passcode protection for secure access.
  • Turbo Mode for fast refresh rates.
  • Works on iOS and Android — fully cross-platform.
  • Compatible with all OBD-II & EOBD compliant vehicles worldwide.
  • Low-energy Bluetooth (BLE) — plug and play, no pairing required.

Device Requirements

iOS: iPhone 4s or later, iPad 3rd gen or later, iPad mini or later, iPod touch 5th gen or later.

Android: Android 4.3+ with BLE support.

Why Choose LELink?

The EngineLink app turns your smartphone into a fully customizable automotive diagnostic tool.

Benefits

  • Run emission tests
  • Monitor live vehicle data
  • Read & clear OBD fault codes
  • Sensor data and performance tracking
  • No subscriptions or in-app purchases
  • Mode 6 diagnostics

EngineLink Features

  • View and reset DTC / CEL fault codes
  • Graph real-time engine data
  • Real-time MPG plotting
  • Heads-up display (HUD) mode
  • Transmission temperature support (vehicle-dependent)
  • Horsepower, torque, boost/vacuum, RPM, coolant, fuel, and more
  • Customizable dashboard layouts
  • Full custom PID and formula support
  • Preloaded standard, GM, Ford, and Prius PID libraries
  • Multiple ECU and CAN bus type support
  • Multiframe data support
  • Export logs via e-mail
  • 0–60 mph and quarter-mile timers

Vehicle Compatibility

LELink supports all OBD-II and EOBD compliant vehicles sold worldwide, including cars, trucks, hybrids, and EV models with supported PID data.
